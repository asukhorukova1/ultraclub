    • The Ultra Club is an alternative-economy design agency. We work at the intersection of design, art and performance. The aim is to find new, self-determined forms of working together by addressing questions of sustainability, ethics, experiment and creativity in design.
    As the working field of design is in transition, we want to create a space to question what is there, a space for exchange and reflection, and look for possible alternatives – ideally together with our clients. Do we have to give up radicalism and experimt to make a living? Do we have to corrupt our ethics to be successful? The Ultra Club is the attempt to finding alternatives to the predominant working conditions in design and elsewhere, to reframe the roles of client and designer so as to enable us and our clients to make a choice on how and with whom we want to work.
    We do not ask for money but we ask for strong ideals. We aim aim at collaborating on the level of shared values – they are the basis for establishing trust, respect and building a reputation for future prospects. Therefore we only work with clients that are curious and brave and that are willing to enter into a dialogue with us
    The Ultra Club is a design office – but also a research-project, a trial-version, a collaboration, a school, a series of events and an experimental laboratory. Our hope is that we can broaden our views, become stronger in our work and that we find collaborators that will challenge our ideals.
