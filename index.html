Zusjes Vintage Boetiek

in collaboration with Rebecca Rui, Theresa Scherrer & Toni Brell

Sanne and Moniek are since April 2016 the Zusjes from Boekerstraat. And although it seems obvious this shop is a family business, they are actually 2 friends sharing love for vintage fashion, Jugendstil, floral wallpaper and glitter. What is remarkable about their shop itself is their sense of scheming colors and patterns rather than separating their fashion strictly into dresses, trousers or hats. For the collaborative project with the shop Toni, Rebecca and Theresa decided to document Sanne and Monieks first selection by capturing every single piece and binding is as a book, the lookbook of Zusjes.

REMAR Second Hand Furniture

in collaboration with Rebecca Rui, Theresa Scherrer & Toni Brell

Remar is a christian organization existing of men and women working voluntarily in reselling furniture that is partly bought for little money from abroad or donated. What i s remarkable about this organization is, that almost the whole profit they make is reinvested in social programs mostly in Africa and Syria. Remar (Rehabilitation of Socially Marginalized People) is currently active in over 70 countries worldwide and is among other working with child care, elderly programs rehabilitation, healthcare humanitarian aid and free meal centers. With the saying „one man’s trash is another one’s treasure“ the collaborative work of Toni, Rebecca and Theresa started to take action. By choosing several objects from their shop in Boekerstraat and putting them visually in a different context, they were both embracing the fact of second hand life and the fact, that religion is bonded to the action.

Little Smile Makers
De Ultraclub is een non-profit reclamebureau, dat zich tot doel heeft gesteld hernieuwde belangstelling te genereren voor de Boekhorststraat in Den Haag. Het eerste project van de Ultraclub is de BoekhorstKrant, een publicatie over de winkels van de Boekhorststraat gemaakt in nauwe samenwerking met 42 van haar winkeliers. Deze krant heeft naast zijn zelfstandige publicitaire functie ook het gesprek geopend tussen de winkeliers en de Ultraclub waaruit een reeks nieuwe projecten zijn voortgekomen die de winkeliers of de omgeving op een concrete manier helpen door middel van grafisch ontwerp.

De Ultraclub is een project van tweede-en derdejaars studenten grafisch ontwerp van de Koninklijke Academie van Beeldende Kunsten in Den Haag.
